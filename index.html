Movie Facts:

  • SIX PERFORMERS WERE OPERATING EACH CREATURE SIMULTANEOUSLY.
  • HENSON MODELED THE SKEKSIS ON THE SEVEN DEADLY SINS.
  • HENSON WROTE THE MOVIE'S ORIGINAL OUTLINE WHILE SNOWED IN AT A HOTEL.
